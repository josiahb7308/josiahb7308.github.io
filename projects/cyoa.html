CYOA Project

This project is a series of links and files with a story that has multiple endings, in this story you are a mafia boss with the goal to be the richest mafia and most sucessful. Different decisions you pick will leave you with a different outcome.

Some challenges that would normally be problematic but we were able to avoid were merge conflicts but since we divided the work to make it so none of us would be on the same 2 lines we didnt have to deal with that. One probelem we encountered a few times was pushing at the same time or trying to push without pulling what our teammate pushed. We fixed this by communicating after we pushed and telling each other when to pull.
